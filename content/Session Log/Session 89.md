# Deliverance from the Darkness
## Retrieving the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md)
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) enter a chamber containing the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). Five orc warriors and an orc spellcaster are inside the chamber.
- A combat ensues. During the encounter, [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) notices that [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md) and the altar it is on fits together perfectly; this suggests to [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) that the two objects are complementary in nature. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) also observes that the orc spellcaster is in the middle of channeling the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) manages to pry away the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) falls unconscious. 
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) throws the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md) to [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and administers a greater [Potion of Healing](../../Potion%20of%20Healing.md) to [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md).
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) uses the [Shadow Sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md) to teleport back to [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md).
## Back to [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md)
### Over the Shadows
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) teleport to the same spot in [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md), which is just outside the main gatehouse of [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) hears fighting from within the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s outer walls. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) observes the army of [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) and deduces that running through them is out of the question.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) casts polymorph on [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and turns him into a quetzalcoatlus, a huge flying dinosaur.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) flies [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) over the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) army without any threat. They land on the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) balcony where [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) welcomes them.
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) hugs [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) sees [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md), [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) at the dining room adjacent to the balcony. [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) explains that [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) is leading the defense of the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) asks the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) if they retrieved the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) shows his father the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) motions to have the portal room fixed.
## A Threat Appears
### Prisoner Negotiations
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) senses a surge of magical energy emanating from the gatehouse. They see a figure looking at them. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) realizes that the man in front of them is [Elvin Claymore](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md).
- [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) speaks in a booming voice:
> *"Bring me your prisoners, or face the consequences."*
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) rushes to fix the portal room, leaving [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) to negotiate with [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md).
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) talks to [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) through [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md)'s magic. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) pleads for time to prepare the hostages.
### Fixing the Portal Room
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) enter the portal room, which is two floors below the balcony and dining room. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) removes the broken pieces of the [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md) from the central pedestal and puts the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md) on it. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) channels magic onto the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md); [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) aids [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) by casting stone shape on the pedestal to make it fit the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md)'s shape. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) tells his two companions that the process will take at least five minutes. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) leaves the two to inform the others.
- On her way out, [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) meets with [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) and discusses with her briefly about the plan.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) delays the negotiation. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) arrives at the dining room and communicates telepathically with [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md). She informs [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) about the time required to fix the portal room.
- [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) sends a warning; as a show of force, [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) hurls meteors and directs it towards the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) decides to give up [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). He, [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) escort the two prisoners to the castle gate.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) sees [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) retrieve the gem shard from his locket and put it on the pedestal. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) asks [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) to tell him about their destination. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) describes the location they want to go to vaguely. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) tells [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) that a unique object or person could help triangulate the destination of the teleportation spell. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) tells [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) to hone in on [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), who is familiar with the magical signature of [Ymerald Gems](../Lore/Ymerald%20Gem.md), targets the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md) as the teleportation's destination.
### Improvising
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) informs [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) about the situation. Using [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md)'s sending spell, [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) asks [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) to stall for more time.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) walks [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) through the open field between the castle and the gatehouse; the meteors phase through the castle as if they are illusory objects.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) formulates a plan; [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) tells [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) that she will provide him with cover to help him with his plan.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) tells [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) that he has set the portal room's teleportation spell. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) tells [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) that he will accompany the first batch of [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) who will be teleported out of [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- The first batch, consisting of [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and most of the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md), teleport out of the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) finagles his way out of his mouth cover and talks to [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) discreetly. [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) pleads to [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) that he would rather be killed than be given back to [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks if he still has affinity for [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md); [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) did not respond, but [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) gleans newfound sincerity from [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md)'s old friend.
- [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) disapproves of [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md)'s suggestion. [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) runs towards [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md).
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) all wait for [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md)'s casting of hallucinatory terrain. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) fails to cast the spell as it needed a lot more time to cast---a fact which [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) disregarded.
- Sensing a problem, [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) pursues his plan. With a dagger, he methodically slashes [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and pierces his chest. Then, he casts dimension door, bringing [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md)'s bloodied body with him. In truth, [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) only faked killing [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md), an act that everyone in the field, including [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md), believed.
- Enraged with the apparent death of [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md), [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) casts meteor swarm and targets the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) with it.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md), [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md), [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and the remaining members of the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) enter the portal room and teleport out of [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- As the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) is destroyed, [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) feel their connection to [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md) weakened; [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md)'s [Shadow Sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md) turns inert.
