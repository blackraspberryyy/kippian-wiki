# Negotiation in the Shadows
## Post-battle Rest
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) observes a temporal anomaly initiated by the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [Shadow Hydra](../../Shadow%20Hydra.md) dissipates while the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) returns to its undamaged state.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) casts cure wounds on [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) and charges the [Bloodstone Arcane Gem](../../Bloodstone%20Arcane%20Gem.md) with a haste spell. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) takes the [Bloodstone Arcane Gem](../../Bloodstone%20Arcane%20Gem.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) then takes a long rest.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) check on [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) eats rations with [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) while [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) inspects [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md)'s binds.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) persuades [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) to change sides. [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) is indifferent and stands by his decisions. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) promises [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) that he will not let others harm him; however, if [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) endangers anyone, [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) will not protect him any further.
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) remembers [Agatha](../NPCs/Agatha.md) gifting her an [Enchor's Arrowhead](../Items/Quest%20Items/Enchor's%20Arrowhead.md). [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) fondly reminisces her time with [Agatha](../NPCs/Agatha.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md) get an intimate moment together.
## Another Intruder
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) alarms the others about an intruder. A [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) enters the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) talks to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) asks the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) to help them defend the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) from intruders. The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) agrees to help, but only if the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) give up [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md).
- The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) claims that [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) contacted it using a sending spell.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) take a minute to discuss the terms. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) bring out [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) to interrogate them.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) casts zone of truth. [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) fails the check and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) succeeds. [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) claims that the [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) are compromised; they are the reason why [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md) has access to the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md).
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) slyly deceives the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) to keep the two prisoners. The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) agrees but only if the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) part with the [Aureate Rapier](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Aureate%20Rapier.md).
- The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) becomes adamant: the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) must either give up the two prisoners or [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md)'s [Aureate Rapier](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Aureate%20Rapier.md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to ready their weapons; he feels that they are being invaded soon.
