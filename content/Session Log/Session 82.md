# Exit Strategy
## No Way Out
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) shares to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that they cannot teleport out of the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) decide to seek wisdom from [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) and [Spekid](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Spekid%20Frostborn.md) using the contact other plane spell.
### Consulting Past Heroes
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks how to teleport out of the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). 
	- Instead of a verbal communication, the contacted beings answer through visions. Moreover, the visions project in front of [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), allowing the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to see [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s visions.
	- The first vision shows two artifacts held by [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) and [Spekid](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Spekid%20Frostborn.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) determine that one of the artifacts is the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md), while the other one is a spherical version of it.
	- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) finds traces of the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md) on the spherical artifact; he concludes that a way out might involve using the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md)'s latent power.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks if the spherical artifact is inside the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md).
	- The vision responds that the artifact is not inside the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks how to get to a certain location from within the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) using the location's sigils.
	- The vision shows [Spekid](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Spekid%20Frostborn.md) and [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) poring over tomes. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) reads the plane shift spell on the spellbook. [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) and [Spekid](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Spekid%20Frostborn.md) try to imbue the plane shift spell onto the inert [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks how to use the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md) to teleport from the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md) to the [Material Plane](../Locations/Planes/Material%20Plane.md).
	- The vision focuses on the gem as [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) casts the teleportation spell, and [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) teleports to the location of the spherical version of the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks about the location of the spherical artifact.
	- The vision shows [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) giving the spherical artifact to a member of the [Acquermann Clan](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md).
	- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) figures out that the vision happened at the [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md) castle he once saw in his vision.
## Invasion
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) spots subtle movement of a statue. He pulls [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) nearer to the statue, and both of them investigate.
- A teleportation circle appear on the floor near the statue. Eight orcs teleport into the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). 
- During the fight, [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) sees [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) looking suspiciously at [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) preemptively attacks [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) squash the orcish invasion.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) asks [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) to stop attacking [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) ceases his assault.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) heals the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) using a dominated orc shaman.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) recognizes the connection between the invading orcs and the orcs that [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) was fighting back in [Opidys](../Locations/Kingdoms/Opidys.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) determines that the orc shaman was not the one attuned to the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
