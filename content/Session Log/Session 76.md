# Sanctioned Treachery
## Temple Exploration
### [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md)'s Excursion
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) goes to the upper floor and sees two pairs of footprints leading deeper into the temple.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) spots five decorative plaques on the wall. Each plaque contains a hieroglyphic image and an inscription written in primordial. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) copies the inscriptions on each plaque.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) catches up to [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and informs her about what the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) has uncovered. The two descends and joins the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md).
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) asks [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md) to translate the inscriptions she found. The five inscriptions tell the following:
> **First inscription:** "[The Old Gods](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md) trapped the void from the otherworld."
> **Second Inscription:** "From the ether, the dragons burst forth. [The Old Gods](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md) trapped them in a desolated plane."
> **Third Inscription:** "The beasts and monsters waged war against the living world."
> **Fourth Inscription:** "The primal races are born. Scorned and cursed, the orcs, one of the primal races, brought fire and destruction upon the world."
> **Fifth Inscription:** "And thus came the [Age of Humans](../Lore/Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Humans.md)."
### The Statues
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) go on the unexplored side of the upper floor. The scholars and [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), who was closely guarded by [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), follow behind them.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter a room and see two sets of statues on either side of a hall. The first set of statues depict [The Old Gods](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md) and [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md). The second set depicts a five-headed dragon, [Miledu](../../Miledu.md), and four broken statues. The two sets of statues appear to be fighting the other.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) investigate the four broken statues. The four statues depict a monstrous beast, a creature with many eyes, a humanoid figure, and an orc.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) exits the hall and finds a door leading to the fountain's source. The two sets of footprints lead to the door. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) receives a premonition that saves [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) from disintegration which triggered when she was trying to open the door.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) casts dispel magic on the door and successfully dispels the ward. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter the room.
### Confronting [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md)
- The room contains a spring filling into a pool of water. The water from the source is tainted with blood and ichor.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) spots a teleportation circle. [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and four orcs appear on the portal. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) attacks [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md).
- [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) raises the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md); he casts time stop and holds the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) in stasis for 12 seconds. During the spell's duration, [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) pulls [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md).
- [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) telepathically asks [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) whether she would promise to help him resurrect his wife. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) reluctantly tells him that she would help him.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) manages to take away the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) from [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). He then throws the [scepter](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) to [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md).
- [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) finds a spell focus and attempts to cast teleport. Before the spell's casting finishes, [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) plunges a dagger onto [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md).
