# Flaws
## Lost Exit
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) shares that the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) is slowly manifesting a magical barrier originating from a divine source.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) takes the time to pray to their respective deities.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) receives a sending message from [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md):
> *"Many orcs here. How long do you need? [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) is wounded, can you take her and [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) in? We are inside the circle."*
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) replies:
> *"We are spent. Need back-up to fight enemies. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) under siege. [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) compromised and attacked us. Bring [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) in."*
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) urges [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) to retrieve both [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md). [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) detects [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md)'s presence inside the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md)'s circle. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) talks to [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) through the statue, but the communication is delayed by 10 seconds. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) shares that they need twelve more hours (1.2 hours in the [Material Plane](../Locations/Planes/Material%20Plane.md)) to complete [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md)'s hallow spell; [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) is surprised. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) detects distortion in [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md)'s voice.
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) tells [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) to take [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md). [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) opts to defend the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md) from enemies.
- After 10 minutes, an exhausted [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and a wounded [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) appear near a statue. [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) appears to be looking for help, while [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) is immediately healed by [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) discuss whether to help or rescue [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md). They decide to rescue [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md). [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) successfully persuades [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and he channels the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). After a tense ten minutes, [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) teleports into the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md).
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) apologizes to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). She could not defend the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md), the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md)'s way out, and so she thinks that she might have endangered the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) console [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) loses his attunement with the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
## [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md)'s Revenge
- The scholars ask the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) about what transpired. [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md) tells [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) about her fear; [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) comforts her, and promises her and the scholars that his duty will be to protect them.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) notices [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md)'s face showing fear. She draws a dagger and walks into the chamber holding [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) persuades [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) to stand down.
- [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) asks the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) why they did not kill [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md). [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) feels betrayed by the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md), and she justifies killing [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md). [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) appears confuse; then, with sudden clarity, [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) accuses the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that they have been tricked by [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md).
- [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) walks towards [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) but [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) stops her. [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) assures [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that she will not harm [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md).
- [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) roughs up [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), then checks his binds. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) spots [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) removing something from [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md)'s finger: a quicksilver ring with a green gemstone.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) asks [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) where he get the ring from; [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) answers that he received it from [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) analyze the ring and trace its origin back to [Sumber](../Locations/Continents/Sumber.md). They realize that the ring descended from [Sumber](../Locations/Continents/Sumber.md)'s [Eladrins](../Factions/The%20Eladrin.md).
- [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) pleads to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to kill [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), but the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) pushes back on the idea. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) keep [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) away from [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md).
- [Professor Aufel](../NPCs/Aufel%20Fernquill.md) casts identify on the ring, and reveals it to possess powerful enchantment magic. [Professor Aufel](../NPCs/Aufel%20Fernquill.md) returns the ring to  [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) gives the [Ring of Ela's Gemcharm](../Items/Quest%20Items/Ring%20of%20Ela's%20Gemcharm.md) to [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) attunes to the [Ring of Ela's Gemcharm](../Items/Quest%20Items/Ring%20of%20Ela's%20Gemcharm.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) takes a short rest, while [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) finishes her long rest.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) informs the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that he only needs six more hours to finish casting the hallow spell.
## A Questionable Trespasser
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) notices an individual approaching the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) ready themselves.
- The intruder's shadowy trappings dissipate as he enters the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) realize that the intruder is [Korvin Acquermann](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), the long-lost brother of [Kayriel](../NPCs/Kayriel%20Acquermann.md) and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md).
> *"I think it is time to reveal myself. I am [Korvin Acquermann](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md). I heard that you are searching for me, [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md)..."*
- [Korvin Acquermann](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) talks to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) whether he knows who is sieging [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) produces a sly smile; he answers that it was him who is assaulting [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s domain.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) reacts and casts hold person on [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
- [Korvin Acquermann](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) admits that he ordered the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) to negotiate with the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). He tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) might provide answers where [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) is.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) asks [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) about his allegiance to [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md). [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) shares that he used [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) and [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md) to further his own goal. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) appeared to help [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) in his bid to free [Miledu](../../Miledu.md), as he thought that the plan was impossible. [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md)'s string of successes worries [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), and now [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) appeals to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to join forces and defeat [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md) and [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md) for good.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) asks [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) about his goals. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) promises to depose the gods and free the people of the world from their hold.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) ask [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) about their lineage. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) tells them that the [Acquermann Clan](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md) was the leader of the first humans who waged war against the gods during the [Age of Humans](../Lore/Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Humans.md), making the [Acquermann Clan](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md) as the 'most flawed clan.' After being defeated, [Akenatun](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Akenatun.md) forgives the [Acquermann Clan](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md) and appoints them to protect his legacy. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) claims that this magnanimous act of mercy is just a show---an attempt by the gods to control people and retain power. This makes the gods flawed, in [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s point of view.
