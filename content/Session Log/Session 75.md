# Temple Raid
## The Temple Underneath
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) discuss about their predicament on whether to rest or not. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) decide to not rest and continue exploring. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) casts mass cure wounds to heal the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) scouts ahead. He tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that the first two chambers inside are empty and safe.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enters the dungeon. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) deduces the age of the masonry to be about 1500 years old. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) sees resemblance between the chamber and the [Wayraven Catacombs](../Locations/Buildings/Wayraven%20Catacombs.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) investigate and conclude that the dungeon was once used as a temple.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) asks [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) about the temple, and she tells them that the priests of the [Temple of The Sealed Path](../Locations/Buildings/Temple%20of%20The%20Sealed%20Path.md) know more about it. [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) knows the temple under the [Temple of The Sealed Path](../Locations/Buildings/Temple%20of%20The%20Sealed%20Path.md) exists, but she has never been inside it.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) investigate the central fountain. A silver tube connects it to the ceiling. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), due to their background, know that the water is a font of holy power. However, blood and ichor currently taint the water.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) discovers a room containing urns of powdered silver, intended to be used to sanctify the water into holy water. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) pours two urns on the fountain and then utters a holy chant. The impurities flow out of the fountain. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), and [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) fill their waterskins with holy water. A few minutes later, the blood and ichor resurface, emanating from the center of the fountain.
### Teleportation Circle
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and the scholars see a teleportation circle drawn on the floor of an adjacent circular room. The scholars disagree on where the sigils point. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) determines that the teleportation circle should teleport them to the [Plane of Dragons](../Locations/Planes/Plane%20of%20Dragons.md).
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) sees a statue on the room next to it. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) realizes that it is the ancestor from his visions. An inscription on the bottom, in ancient primordial, reads [Krynn Acquermann](../../Krynn%20Acquermann.md).
### Pillars
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) see two pillars containing hieroglyphs detailing events concerning [Akenatun](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Akenatun.md) and [Matunda](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Matunda.md). 
### Scrolls
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) sees a scroll rack and pockets 10 empty scrolls. She sees a ramp sloping upwards, and decides to proceed to the temple's upper floor.
### [Roldan Vinke](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), Imprisoned
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) checks a nearby hallway harboring two unopened doors. He spots three sets of footprints, two sets coming in and out of the right door.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) opens the right door and reveals a used storeroom converted into a camping spot.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) hears a voice from within the locked left door. The voice asks [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) to set him free. He destroys the doorknob and sees [Roldan Vinke](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) running towards the door. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) slams the door, breaking the hinges and trapping [Roldan Vinke](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) puts on manacles on [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md).
- Other members of the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) rush to [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and confront [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) sees [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) in a deplorable state. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), sensing vulnerability from [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), asks everyone to leave the room so [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) would confide in her.
- [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) tells [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) about his reason for allying himself with [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md): [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) will do everything to be with his dead wife. [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md) promised [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) just that, but because of their sudden treachery, [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) is now second guessing their intents. He promises to help the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) if they help him revive her wife.
- [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) exits the room, and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) shares [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md)'s story with the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) then looks for [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), sensing something amiss with [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), concocts a plan with [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) casts zone of truth, prompting a thorough interrogation on [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md).
- [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) retells his story to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) reveals his wife's name: [Kara Vinke](../NPCs/Kara%20Vinke.md).
