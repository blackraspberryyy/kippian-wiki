# A Divided Approach
## [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) Finds a New Path
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) perceives a change in his [Shadow Sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md). The [sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md)'s shadowy camouflage is removed and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) sees two spell circles vying for space on the [sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) needs to choose one of the two spell circles that will permanently manifest on the [sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks for [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s guidance.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) determines that the spell circle will trigger a teleport circle spell that targets a place in [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) picks the teleportation aspect of the [Shadow Sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md).
## Decisions With No Reservations
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) talks to [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) is unmoved by [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md)'s words, but he places his life on the paladin's hands. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) pledges to save [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md)'s life.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) are called by voices originating from the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md). They discern the voices to be from [Spekid](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Spekid%20Frostborn.md) and [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md). The voices call on the three to make a decision: untether the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) from the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md) or allow the hallowed place to remain attached to the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md).
- The three choose the first option: remove the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) away from the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md) and the [Plane of Shadows](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). By doing this, the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) will become inaccessible to all beings.
- The [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) is beset by intense tremors, and the hallowed place is detached from the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) puts her hand out of the entryway; she pierces a magical film and experiences frostbite.
## The [Party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) Splits Up
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) decides to use the [Shadow Sigil](../../Shadow%20Sigil.md) to teleport to a place he saw in his visions: [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s domain in [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) plan to go with [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md). [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) follows [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md). [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) join [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) is persuaded to use the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md) to teleport to the [Material Plane](../Locations/Planes/Material%20Plane.md). [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and the scholars follow [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md). Only one more individual can join [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) wants to follow [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), but he cannot bring [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) with him due to the two teleport spells' limitations. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) consults his patron deity, [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md).
- [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md) pleads for [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md)'s help. He tells [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) that his oath to [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) can be entrusted to other people.
> *"[Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md)... I require your strength."*
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) decides to entrust [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) to [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md).
