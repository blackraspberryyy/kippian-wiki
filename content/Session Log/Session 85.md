# Shades of War
## Fallen Shadows
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md), [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md), [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) teleport to [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) sees the walled castle from his vision.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) spots shadows on the ground. As the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) approach the [castle](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) gatehouse, the shadows gain volume and turn to [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). The [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) overwhelm the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) run towards the gatehouse. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) persuades the people inside to open the gate for them by telling him that he is the son of [Kristofer Acquermann](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter the gatehouse and escape the [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md)' assault. They meet seven battle-worn [Knights of the Wayraven](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) who are dejected and tired. 
- The [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md)' captain introduces himself as [Rudyard](../NPCs/Rudyard%20Flick.md).[Rudyard](../NPCs/Rudyard%20Flick.md) remembers [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and reminisces a time before the war.
- [Rudyard](../NPCs/Rudyard%20Flick.md) sends a knight to fetch [Kristofer Acquermann](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). He explains that the [Knights of the Wayraven](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) have been defending the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) for twelve years. [Kristofer Acquermann](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) has led the defense.
- [Rudyard](../NPCs/Rudyard%20Flick.md) learns that [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) has died. He alludes that their enemy's death changes [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s plans. [Rudyard](../NPCs/Rudyard%20Flick.md) sends someone to get [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) rests as they wait for [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md).
## An [Acquermann](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md) Reunion
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) enters the gatehouse's side entrance and stares at [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) acts paranoid, and accuses that the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md)'s presence is one of [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s many tricks.
- Before the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) is able to explain themselves, the shadows assault the gatehouse. A [Shadow Golem](../../Shadow%20Golem.md) rises from the ground and attacks [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md), with help from [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), defeat the [Shadow Golem](../../Shadow%20Golem.md). 
## [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s Plan
- After witnessing both [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md)'s and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md)'s powers, [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) finally trusts his children. He apologizes to the two and invites [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) to the [castle](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
> *"Forgive me if I was paranoid earlier. Years of war changed me."*
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) explain that they know that [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) is in [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md), and that their plan was to lift the siege on the [castle](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- As they are walking towards the castle, [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), who was once banished, ended in [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) and the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) defeat [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) decided to reside in [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) reveals his plan to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). He tells them that his plan was to bait [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) and [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md) in sieging [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) explains that he halted communication with [Midkip](../Locations/Settlements/Midkip.md) in order to preserve their forces and protect their kin.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) explains that his plan works because [The Cult of Miledu](../../The%20Cult%20of%20Miledu.md) needs to remove [Miledu](../../Miledu.md)'s bindings, one of which is sealed within the [castle](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- Because of what the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) told [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) tells them that the status quo has changed, and his plan must adapt to it.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) notices a faint, emerald-hued light emanating from [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s locket.
### [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md) (Or What's Left of It)
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md), [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) arrive at the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) leads the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to the portal sanctum.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter an empty room save for a pedestal containing the destroyed remains of a spherical artifact. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) immediately recognizes that it is the [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md), which they saw from [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s magic.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) confesses that in order to make his plan foolproof, [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) destroyed the [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md). This drastic action makes the binding permanent on the [castle](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s end. But the artifact also enables the [castle](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s portal sanctum; this means that [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) has no way to teleport out of [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). He hides this fact from the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) in order to not weaken their morale.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) shows his locket, revealing an Ymerald shard that was once part of the [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) asks [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) if he knows where the rest of their party is located considering that the location of the [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md) should have been their destination. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) claims that he knows nothing about where their [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) are right now.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) asks if the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) knows a way out of [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md), but learns that the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) depended on him to get them out of [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md).
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) asks if [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) knows about the other places that bind [Miledu](../../Miledu.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) knows about the following: [Grand Cathedral](../Locations/Buildings/Grand%20Cathedral.md), [Temple of Akenatun and Matunda](../../Temple%20of%20Akenatun%20and%20Matunda.md), [Temple of The Sealed Path](../Locations/Buildings/Temple%20of%20The%20Sealed%20Path.md), and the one in [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s portal sanctum.
