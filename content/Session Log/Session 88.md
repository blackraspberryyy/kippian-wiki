# Acts of Blasphemy
## Enter the [Underground Temple of Macchi](../Locations/Buildings/Underground%20Temple%20of%20Macchi.md)
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) teleport to a hot, cramped room that smells strongly of iron. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) notices that dried blood is splattered all over the dark-colored bricks that make up the floor and walls.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) realizes the room's construction and design is similar to [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md)'s temples in [Opidys](../Locations/Kingdoms/Opidys.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) casts arcane eye to scout the area. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) sees four mindless orcs, similar to those the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) battled at the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md), walk up a flight of stairs. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s arcane eye follows the orcs.
- At the top of the stairs is another orc who slashes the palms of the four orcs. Blood drips from the orcs as they enter another room. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) spots a stone slab and a spiral staircase that leads [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s arcane eye back to the floor where they teleported in.
### Encounter: Orc and Blood
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) casts greater invisibility on herself and sneaks her way to grapple the solitary orc. [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) fails and combat ensues.
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) take down the orc. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) checks a room and sees objects and trinkets bearing [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md)'s symbols; seeing the discarded articles dishearten [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md).
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) investigates the stone slab and sees an inscription in primordial that he cannot read. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) points [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) to the passageway where the four orcs went through.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) asks [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) to translate the inscription. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) surges through the next room and finds a fountain filled with blood. The four orcs beside the fountain are letting their blood flow into the pool of blood. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) pushes the orcs away from the fountain. 
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) casts comprehend languages and reads the inscription on the stone slab:
> **Let strength pave the way.**
- A creature created from blood emerges from the fountain of blood and attacks [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md). [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) kill the solitary orc; [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) keeps the dagger used by the orc. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) rush in to help [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md). [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) learns that the fountains were once used to contain holy water; now it contain orcish blood.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) finds two more fountains filled with blood.  [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) follow the rest of the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) attack the [Blood Golem](../../Blood%20Golem.md). [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) and [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) destroy the fountain, allowing the blood leak out onto the dungeon floor. The act severely weakens the [Blood Golem](../../Blood%20Golem.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) removes the [Blood Golem](../../Blood%20Golem.md)'s tether to the blood, and the creature dies.
- Two more [Blood Golem](../../Blood%20Golem.md) rise from the two fountains; the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) defeat them in large part due to [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) destroying the fountains by casting stone shape and earth tremor.
## Beginning of the End
### [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) Lacks Strength
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) share what they saw on the stone slab. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) approach it and they feel intense divine aura emanating from the other side of the stone slab. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) inspects the stone slab and see it nudge.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) decides to push the slab. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) sees a vision that if [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) succeeds, it would trigger the events that lead to his vision.
- Despite help from [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) fails to push the slab. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) feels defeated and a bit betrayed. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) console [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) asks for some time to think. He asks [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) to retrieve [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md)'s articles from the nearby room.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) return to the chamber where the fountains are located. They see a large ornate stone door near the fountain and a regular stone door opposite the door they entered from.
### Exploring the [Underground Temple of Macchi](../Locations/Buildings/Underground%20Temple%20of%20Macchi.md)]]
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) check the large door and sees four verses written in different languages. He reads the inscription:
> Herein lies [Miledu](../../Miledu.md), bound and in deep repose.
> 
> Twelve chains bind the harbinger of doom.
> 
> [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md)'s boundless strength locks the chains.
> 
> Heed! [Miledu](../../Miledu.md)'s unbinding will herald a new age, one of destruction and darkness.
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) survey the other passageway. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) uses the arcane eye to check the room. He sees two rectangular pools of milky white watery substance, each harboring a humanoid body. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) also sees another door and a drape where a door was once located. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) motions the arcane eye to enter the drapes, and the arcane eye dissipates.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) reform and discuss their options. They decide to check out the chamber scouted by [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter the room. Immediately, [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) recognizes that the rectangular pools are not part of the temple's original design.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) enters the door and finds a living quarter complete with a bed, several pieces of wooden furniture, and a desk. Three magic items catch [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md)'s attention: a belt bearing [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md)'s symbol, a stout candle, and a necklace with an amber pendant.
### Empty Vessels
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) investigates the milky white substance and he determines that it is used to preserve bodies. Its arcane use can be attributed to necromancy magic. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) casts detect thoughts on the two bodies, but the spell yield no result.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) enters the pool and pulls up the submerged body. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) is surprised to find out that it is the exact clone of [Elix Claymore](../../Elix%20Claymore.md).
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) retrieves the other body; [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) and [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) realizes that the body resembles that of [Elvin Claymore](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md), only much younger.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) weigh in on what to do with the bodies. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) asks [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) for help identifying the three magic items she retrieved from the nearby room.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) determines the following:
	- The belt contains [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md)'s power.
	- The necklace is a periapt that counters poison.
	- The inscription on the candle lists the name of [The Old Gods](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) also determines that the three magic items, the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md), [Ring of Ela's Gemcharm](../Items/Quest%20Items/Ring%20of%20Ela's%20Gemcharm.md), [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md), [Matunda's Mace](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Matunda's%20Mace.md) exhibit the form of magic. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) deduces a connection between them.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) decide to burn the bodies. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) reenter the room to find flammable objects. As she was pulling the wooden furniture, she discovers a used spell scroll. She asks [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) to investigate it. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) learns that the contingency spell was the spell that was inscribed on the spell scroll.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) burns the two bodies devoid of life and consciousness. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) offers his prayers while [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) mutters a vengeful elegy in front of the body resembling [Elix Claymore](../../Elix%20Claymore.md).
