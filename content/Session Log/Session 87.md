# Truth, Magic and Chaos
## Concocting a New Plan
### One Way Out
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) talks to [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md). They enter the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s dining hall and discuss a new plan.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) concludes that their only lead for a way out of [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md) is [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md). [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) tells them that [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) has a hideout a mile outside the walls, but it is surrounded by [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) casts arcane eye to scout the area and look for the hideout.
### [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md)'s Patrol
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) investigates the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s perimeter. He realizes that the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) is a defensible position.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) realizes that the trinket containing [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s soul is pulsating with energy.
### Resolving Familial Matters
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) attempts to talk to [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), but [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) preferred being alone and exits the dining hall. [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) follows [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md).
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) asks [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) about his feelings for [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) tells her that he is hurt by their father's actions. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) overhears this, and approaches [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) to apologize.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) react coldly to [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s appeal. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) concedes, and asks the two to at least give him a chance to prove himself.
### Preparations
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) informs the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that she found the hideout. The hideout is actually a ruined catacombs, but the entrance is guarded by many [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). A brute force or a stealthy approach is out of the question.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) devise a new plan: the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) will provide distraction so that [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) can slip through and investigate [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s hideout.
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s distraction requires him to destroy the walls and let the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) army inside. The [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) will defend the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
- [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s plan hinges on the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) retrieving the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md) so that they can activate the [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s portal sanctum. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) calculates that the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) can defend the castle for four days.
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) agrees with the plan. She also tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that she, [Leneia](../NPCs/Leneia%20Yarumcy.md) and [Michaela](../NPCs/Michaela%20Randall.md) will help defend the castle. [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) will also be in their care.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) feels daunted by the plan because of her dwindling magic. [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) gives her a scroll that allows [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) to conjure a great feast before them, restoring their health and magic before they proceed with the plan.
## Into [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s Lair
### Sense of Familiarity
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) use the tunnel underneath [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)'s gatehouse to evade the [Shadow Knights](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). After hearing the bell, a pre-approved signal from [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) that the way is clear, the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) emerge from the tunnel and locate catacombs.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter the catacombs. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) notice that the architecture reminds them of the [Wayraven Catacombs](../Locations/Buildings/Wayraven%20Catacombs.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter through a hall lined with statues on both sides. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) inspects the statues, and realizes that the symbols below are the ones they found in the [Wayraven Catacombs](../Locations/Buildings/Wayraven%20Catacombs.md).
### A Dead Elven Knight
- At the end of the hall, they find a desiccated corpse of a male elven knight. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) checks that the corpse has been dead for a year. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) finds some traces of necromancy magic on the body; he realizes that the magic has just been cut off recently. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) realizes the corpse's trappings came from [Sumber](../Locations/Continents/Sumber.md).
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) opens the door and a [Ghostly Knight](../../Ghost%20Knight.md) attacks the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) runs into the next room. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) successfully persuades the ghost to return back to its body.
- The corpse rises from the dead for a brief moment. The dead knight looks at [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), mimes out that [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) did it to him. Then the statue points at a statue.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) investigate the statue and find that it was moved before. They check the wall behind it and find a passageway behind the wall. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) decides not to forcefully open the wall as it might contain traps. The two walk to [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md)'s location.
### [Padwin](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md)'s Riddle
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) finds a room containing six shelves and an unlit lantern at the center. The shelves contain identical scrolls. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) tries to light the lantern, but to no avail. 
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) reads the two tablets on either side of the room:
> **Tablet 1**
> Dedicated to [Padwin Furwish](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md)
> 
> Date interred: AC 163
>
> Friend of the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md)
>
> Founder of [Lorehold College](../../College%20of%20Lorehold.md)
>
> "No darkness can envelop the light of truth."
> 
> **Tablet 2**
> 
> Magical fire radiates revealing light
>
> Luminous light reveals invisible truth
>
> Between truths lie danger, magic then chaos wild
>
> Behind history is a path to absolute truth
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) join [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md). [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) casts sacred flame on the lantern. The lantern lights up and reveals phrases on top of the six shelves:
		1. HISTORY HARBORS TRUTH
		2. DANGER INVITES DEATH
		3. TRUTH ENDANGERS
		4. MAGIC ORDERS CHAOS
		5. CHAOS OBSCURES TRUTH
		6. CHAOS AMPLIFIES DANGER
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) deduces that there must be an entrance behind the first shelf. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) finds a gap and pulls the shelf, revealing a narrow hallway.
### [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s Hiding Spot
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) find [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s study. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) inspects the spell circle on the floor. The spell circle constitutes a part of a spell. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) copies the sigils on the spell circle.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) find tomes, books and scrolls similar to the ones found in the room with the lantern. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) realizes that the series of rooms were once a repository of records before it was repurposed by [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) finds a picture frame of her adoptive mother, [Queen Zwenyr](../NPCs/Zwenyr%20Elthana.md), beside [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s bed.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) opens the scrolls and find them empty. She returns to the room with the lit lantern, and opens a scroll. Ink magically appears on the scroll, allowing [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) to read its content. Aside from historical records, [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) stumbles upon a used plane shift spell scroll. 
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) survey the room with the lantern to find a usable plane shift scroll. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) opens a warded scroll with an explosive rune on it, dealing damage to him and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and knocking some scrolls off the shelves.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) attempts to dispel the magical trap on the passageway behind the statue. He fails and the passageway caves in.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) attempt to find a plane shift spell scroll. As they open the scrolls, they read more about [ Padwin's historical records and insights](../Lore/Padwin's%20Notes.md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) pops [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s trinket and asks him about the riddle. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s soul was unhelpful, but he learns that [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) has a deep love for [Queen Zwenyr](../NPCs/Zwenyr%20Elthana.md).
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) casts speak with the dead on the dead elven knight. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) learns nothing about the riddle, but learns that their name is [Alya Stormrose](../NPCs/Alya%20Stormrose.md). [Alya](../NPCs/Alya%20Stormrose.md) reveals that their body guards the catacombs because [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) requires [ Padwin's knowledge](../Lore/Padwin's%20Notes.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) finally retrieve two plane shift spell scrolls. [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) supplies the sigil sequence and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) casts the spell.
