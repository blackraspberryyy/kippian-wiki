# A Costly Mistake
## Fight Against the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md)
- Sensing deception, the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) refuse to negotiate with the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md), minus [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), prepare to fight the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) warns the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md):
> *"So, you have chosen this path..."*
- During the battle, the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) envelops a part of the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) with magical darkness. The vibrations that [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) felt become clearer: an army, later revealed to consist of skeletons bound by shadow, enter the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md).
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) negates the magic by using the [Driftglobe](../../Driftglobe.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) lead the charge against the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) and the skeleton army. [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md), [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) provide support to the frontline. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) manages to contribute through his spiritual weapon.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) drops the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) to its knees. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) deals the finishing blow to the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md). The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md)'s shadowy veil disappears, revealing a humanoid entity---[Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), who are near the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md), notice it. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) recognizes the face from his dreams: the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) transforms into [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) receives a trinket containing the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md)'s soul. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) cannot glean whether the soul came from a human or nonhuman entity.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) drops dead. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) carries [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) away from the battlefield as [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) finish dropping the remaining skeletons.
## Death and Deceit
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) plans to cast revivify on [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), but he worries that casting the spell might cancel his progress on the hallow spell which is about halfway from finishing. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) wakes up [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) to increase [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md)'s chances on hallow spell.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) successfully revives [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) without compromising the casting of the hallow spell. After a brief rest and a quick discussion, [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) casts revivify on the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md).
- The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) attempts to attack [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) saw through the deception, but only [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) was quick enough to stop the [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md) from attacking and kill it for good.
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) breaks the trinket and asks the soul that was trapped in the trinket:
>  *"What happened to [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)?"*
- The [Shadow Knight](../../Shadow%20Knight.md)'s soul replies:
> *"[Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s domain is being sieged."*
