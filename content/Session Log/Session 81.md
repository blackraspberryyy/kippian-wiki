# [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s Gambit
## Clearing Family Matters
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) talks to the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md). He asks them to help him defeat [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md). [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that he is their only way to get to [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md).
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) asks for the sigils that would take them to [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md)'s location.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) what he will do with [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) replies that he will do anything to stop the [Knights of the Wayraven](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) even if it means killing his own brother. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) adds that the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md)' absence is due to him sieging [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s domain.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) mentions that [Kariil](../../Kariil%20Acquermann.md), [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s son, is dead. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) reacts surprised for a moment, but immediately turns stoic.
> *"What he did, he thought that it was the right thing to do. I have no problems with that."*
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) asks the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) about their plan to get out of here. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) does not share any information; [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) warns them that travel between planes is no trivial matter. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) looks at the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md)'s wizard, [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), and condescendingly asks him if he knows the way out of the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md).
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) approaches a shallow recess on the wall and sits on it. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) tells the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that he will stay there and wait until the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) wants his help.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) approaches [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and tells him that she will respect his decision to take with [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) whatever it may be.
### [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) talk to [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) if he plans to kill [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) reiterates that he does not need to kill [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md), but if that is needed to stop [Akenatun](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Akenatun.md)'s legacy, then he would not hesitate.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) enters the conversation and asks if he would need to kill the [Acquermann Clan](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md) in order to kill the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) and their Lordknight. [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) replies:
> *"Make no mistake, being a Lordknight is not inherited through blood. So if no one takes the mantle, then there would be no killing."*
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) also adds, with disdain, that he will not be the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md)' Lordknight.
### [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md)'s Worries
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md), while talking about [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), spot a restless [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) looking at [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) talking. 
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) sees [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) as guilty and worried about her failure to defend the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) tries to comfort her. [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) asks [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) about their plan to get out.
### [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s Doubts
- [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md) talks to [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) about who just appeared. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) tells her that it was [Kristofer](../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s lost brother, [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
- [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md) worries about what the future holds. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) comforts [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md). 
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) approaches [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and ask him details about the plan to get out of the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) tells [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) that the scholars know more about the details. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) decide to talk to the scholars.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) talk to the scholars about the teleportation spell to get out of the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md). As [Professor Aufel](../NPCs/Aufel%20Fernquill.md) and [Professor Embersense](../NPCs/Dorfir%20Embersense.md) converse, it becomes evident that there was miscommunication between the two: they thought that the other knows the specific spell that could teleport them between planes.
- The professors save face by explaining that they thought that they would go back to the [Material Plane](../Locations/Planes/Material%20Plane.md). Then, a simple teleport spell, which both the professors knew, would suffice to teleport them into [Elvin](../../Elvin%20Claymore.md)'s location.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) gathers the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to divulge the professors' lapse. [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) opts to guard [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
### [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md)
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) pulls [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) away from the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) for a quick talk.
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) shares that he does not want [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s help. [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) sees that [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) is burdened about something. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) shares that he sees himself killing [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md), their uncle.
- [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) tells [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) that she is willing to share [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md)'s burden. If it comes to it, [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) is willing to be the one to kill [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
