# Veiled Eyes and Splitting Heads
## Another Conundrum
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) pulls the dagger that is plunged into [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md)'s back. He realizes that the dagger is from [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) rushes to [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and revives him. [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) becomes stable, but unconscious.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) discuss what to do with [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). The scholars investigate the teleportation circle. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) note that the effects of the telepathic bond spell just ended.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) gives the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) to [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md). [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) notices similar patterns between the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) and the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md). He deduces that the patterns on the surface of the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md) is patterned after the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) is adamant that he wants to talk to [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md). [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) persuades the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) to spare his life.
- The scholars learn that the sigil sequence on the teleportation circle points to a place deep underground.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) decides to enter the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md) and keep [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) there. [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) are bound by [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md).
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md), [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md), [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and the scholars decide to enter the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md). [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) stay outside to guard the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
## Inside [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md)
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) enter the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) glows brightly. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) remembers that the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) is where the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) was retrieved.
- Near the pedestal where the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) is placed is a blank slate. When [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) approaches it, the light from the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) shows hidden inscription on the blank slate. The inscription is written in primordial; [Professor Embersense](../NPCs/Dorfir%20Embersense.md) translates it.
- The inscription reveals the following:
> *To protect the artifact, [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) and [Spekid](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Spekid%20Frostborn.md) housed the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) inside the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). Through the divine power of the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md), the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md) binds the great evil that is [Miledu](../../Miledu.md).*
- A section of the blank slate is reserved for a set of instructions for a cleric of [Matunda](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Matunda.md) or [Akenatun](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Akenatun.md). The instruction includes an inscribed hallow spell; casting it should restore the binding of [Miledu](../../Miledu.md). During this time, [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) says, that he cannot break casting the hallow spell; the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) should defend him at all costs.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) discuss their options, and concludes that putting the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) back is their best course of action. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) casts the hallow spell, and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) exits the [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md) to inform [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md), and to use the [Acquermann](../../Acquermann%20Clan.md) sending stone to inform [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md).
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) puts the [Scepter of Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md) back at its pedestal and casts the hallow spell; [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) realizes that this event sets off the recurring vision he has. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) immediately informs [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) through a statue within the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md).
### Message to [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md)
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) sends the following message to [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md):
> *"New plan, inside [Labyrinthus](../Locations/Areas/Labyrinthus.md), binding [Miledu](../../Miledu.md) using [Scepter Akenatun](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md), enemies could interfere, send help if possible, teleportation circle below [Temple of Sealed Path](../Locations/Buildings/Temple%20of%20The%20Sealed%20Path.md), right now."*
- After receiving [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s warning, [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) enter the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md).
## A Quick Long Rest
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) decide to rest while [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) elects to guard [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md). The scholars, [Elix](../../Elix%20Claymore.md) and [Roldan](../../Roldan%20Vinke.md) rest in an adjacent room.
### Seeing Ahead
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md), [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md), [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md), [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) have a collective vision of them fighting a five-headed creature. The vision also shows their [mythic items](../../Mythic%20Items.md) gaining more power. The vision ends with a blast of fire.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) wake up, and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) notices a teleportation circle appearing at the other end of the hall. A five-headed creature with violet-black scales and red eyes emerge from the teleportation circle.
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) defeats the [Shadow Hydra](../../Shadow%20Hydra.md) and successfully defends [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md).
