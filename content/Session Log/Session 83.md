# Sharing Burdens
## Another Invasion
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) tightens [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s binds. She sees him seething with anger.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) learns that he can channel the hallow spell to negate teleportation into the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) decides to do it.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) comforts [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) who is exhausted from casting the hallow spell.
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) and [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) detect another intrusion. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) ready themselves for another assault.
- After ten minutes, eight orcs teleport into the [Hall of Akenatun](../Locations/Buildings/Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) defeat the invading orcs.
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) determines that the orcs teleported from the temple underneath [Yvernus](../Locations/Areas/Yvernus%20District.md).
### [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s Demise
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) uses the opportunity to escape his binds and to cast a spell that charms [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md). [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) arrives on the scene first.
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) demands that [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) kneel and apologize to him. [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) deceivingly rebuffs him and tells him that he cannot use [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) to do what he want.
- [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) orders [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) to attack [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md); [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) paralyzes [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md). Free from the spell, [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) plunges a dagger into [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
- [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md) tries to negotiate with [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md). [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) stop [Adikia](../PCs/Adikia%20Unalome.md). [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md) dies. 
- [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md) consoles [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md). [Kirren](../NPCs/Kirren%20Acquermann.md) affirms her loyalty to [Kiris](../PCs/Kiris%20Acquermann.md).
- [Arc](../PCs/Arc.md) fashions a trinket from [Korvin](../NPCs/Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s soul.
### [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md)'s Blessing
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) prays to [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md). A vision appears in front of her. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) also sees the divine apparition.
- [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md) tells [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) to be steadfast and assures her about the future.
> *"Do not doubt yourself. Everything will be alright."*
- [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md) walks towards [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md). [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md)'s strength is replenished by the divine presence.
- [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md) blesses the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) and restores their fortitude. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) receives a wish spell from the goddess.
> *"You, [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), displayed immense faith. I am not here only to comfort you, but to tell you that you are blessed by the goddess [Matunda](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Matunda.md) and [Aisef](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aisef.md)."*
- A blinding divine light bursts forth from [Akenatun's Scepter](../../Scepter%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) is enveloped with divine power. [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md)'s divine power is enough to finish the hallow spell that [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) is casting.
