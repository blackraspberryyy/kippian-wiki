# Of Jaws and Gems
## Submerged
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and the four scholars teleport into an air bubble submerged in water.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) casts dancing lights, but the spell immediately dissipates as if it was countered magically. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) casts bless on the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md).
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) uses her [Driftglobe](../../Driftglobe.md) to cast light. [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) spots three glowing lights moving to their direction. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) see that they are on top of glass-like surface with bluish-green streaks. They also see pairs of pillars rise to the surface.
- The lights turn out to be a shark with an inlaid gem on its forehead. The bubble pops and the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) swim upwards.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) attempts to retrieve the [Immovable Rod](../Items/Immovable%20Rod.md) from [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md)'s backpack, but pulls a lantern holding eternal flame. The flame does not die out from being submerged in water. [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) hooks the lantern to him, giving him light.
- The [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md) swallows [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md)'s [Driftglobe](../../Driftglobe.md), then attacks [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md). The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) take turns helping [Dello](../../Dello%20Stoneshard.md), who cannot swim.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), after escaping the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md), casts a spell to reform the stone pillar nearest to him.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) discerns that the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md) is actually a construct. [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md), [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md), and the three scholars surface. They spot a cavern with a large [Ymerald Gem](../Lore/Ymerald%20Gem.md) deposit in the middle of the rocky island held up by the pillars. [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) spots a glowing [Ymerald Gem](../Lore/Ymerald%20Gem.md) on a pedestal near the cave wall.
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) swims towards the [Ymerald](../Lore/Ymerald%20Gem.md) and sees an inscription which she cannot read. [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) remarks that the gem of his [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md) is cut from the mineral deposit.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) reads the inscription that [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) found:
> [!note] Lair Riddle
> To unseal the entrance to my lair,
> Three keys you must prepare,
> One from driftwood, One from sunken stone
> And last a trinket the Ymerald keeper owns
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) spots a wall containing three keyholes and an open cavity. She deduces that it has something to do with the riddle on the inscription.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) escapes the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md)'s bite and surfaces. [Dello](../../Dello%20Stoneshard.md) is saved by the combined efforts of [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md), [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) fight the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md). [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) experiences a magical shockwave emanating from the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md). [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) surfaces, leaving [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) to a grappling contest with the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) conclude that the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md) can absorb magical spells. [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) and [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) concoct a plan to trap the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md) using the [Immovable Rod](../Items/Immovable%20Rod.md).
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) becomes incapacitated and is saved by [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md). [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) traps the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md).
## Befuddled
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) takes a short rest as the scholars prepare the teleport circle spell.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) obtains the gem from the [gemshark](../../Ymerald%20Gemshark%20Golem.md)'s forehead. The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) decide to open [Zagi's Lair](../Locations/Buildings/Zagi's%20Lair.md).
### [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) Contacts [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md)
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks [Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) using the contact other plane spell.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks where the keys are located.
> *"Two inside the hollow, one you must provide"*
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks if the keys are already made.
> *"Yes"*
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks if there is something inside the lair.
> *"Treasure and knowledge"*
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) asks about the last key.
> *"A trinket that represents you"*
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) ask where the two keys are located.
> *"A piece of driftwood floats, a stone sinks"*
### A Larger Ymerald Deposit
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) scours the hollow and finds a key carved from driftwood.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) dive and search for a stone key. The two realize that trapped underneath the glass surface is a much larger mineral deposit.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) finds a stone key.
## [Zagi's Lair](../Locations/Buildings/Zagi's%20Lair.md)
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) opens [Zagi's Lair](../Locations/Buildings/Zagi's%20Lair.md) using the two keys and a driftwood piece from his [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md).
- The [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) see a statue of [Zerrus Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md) holding the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md) and the [Magus Labyrinthus](../Items/Quest%20Items/Magus%20Labyrinthus.md).
- Beside the temple is a portal room, allowing them to teleport to any place as long as they know the location's sigil sequence.
- The inner chamber contains scroll racks, shelves and a desk with a chained spellbook. A folded letter tucked between the book and the desk.
> [!note] [Zagi's Letter to Splix](../Lore/Zagi's%20Letter%20to%20Splix.md)
> To the keeper of my staff,
>
> I now hold you responsible for this large deposit of Ymerald. I have also collected and recorded all that I know in this room. S, I entrust all of these to you.
>
> I foresee an enemy from the heavens... Maybe this extraterrestrial boon is not a gift, but a weapon to protect us against the things to come.
>
> But I digress. S, you and your companions must first defeat the evil that plans to release [Miledu](../../Miledu.md). The twelve chains that bind the harbinger of doom are weakening. But the god is vulnerable from its stasis; strike now before it awakens.
>
> [Zerrus Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md),
> Creator and First Keeper of the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md)
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) retrieves a book [Aulephi](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aulephi.md) and [Yekora](../Deities/New%20Gods/Yekora.md). She hopes that she can use the book to strike peace between the followers of the two deities.
- [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) sees a collection of [Zerrus Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md)'s records about [Opidys](../Locations/Kingdoms/Opidys.md). He enlists the scholars help in copying them and sharing them with Opidian people.
- [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) finds a book detailing [Zerrus Zagi](../Lore/Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md)'s explorations in [Suirsya](../Locations/Continents/Suirsya.md).
### Goodbyes
- The scholars tell [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) and the [party](../Factions/Seven%20Up....md) that they have decided to part ways with them. They plan to teleport to [Effil University](../Locations/Buildings/Savras%20University.md) to report their actions.
- [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md) entrusts his gold to the scholars. He asks them to give it to the [Effil Orphanage](../Locations/Buildings/Effil%20Orphanage.md) and to inform his order about his situation.
- [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md) shares their knowledge about the larger deposit of [Ymerald](../Lore/Ymerald%20Gem.md) deep within [Zagi's Lair](../Locations/Buildings/Zagi's%20Lair.md). They persuade the scholars to not share about the [Ymerald](../Lore/Ymerald%20Gem.md), knowing that there will be an arms race to take it.
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) bids his goodbye to the scholars and [Fiamil](../NPCs/Fiamil%20Underwood.md). The scholars teleport to [Effil University](../Locations/Buildings/Savras%20University.md).
- [Splix](../PCs/Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md) teleports [Thoradin](../PCs/Thoradin%20Goodman.md), [Theobald](../PCs/Theobald%20Clayhollow.md), [Adeena](../PCs/Adeena%20Oberon.md) and [Noah](../PCs/Noah%20Skie.md) to the lair deep within the earth to confront their destiny.
