## Most Recent
- [Session 45](../Session%20Log/Session%2045.md): Two years ago, [Kayriel](Kayriel%20Acquermann.md) broke contact with [Kippian](Kingdom%20of%20United%20Kippian.md) to validate rumors of an individual using the [Acquermann](Acquermann%20Clan.md)'s name. He thought that it would lead him to his missing brothers, [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) or [Kristofer](Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). The thread prompted him to go back to [Kippian](Kingdom%20of%20United%20Kippian.md) to search for clues. [Kayriel](Kayriel%20Acquermann.md) managed to do it as [Frederick](Frederick%20Oberon.md)'s right-hand man.
- [Session 53](../Session%20Log/Session%2053.md): [Kariil Acquermann](Kariil%20Acquermann.md) is supposed to be the son of [Korvin Acquermann](Korvin%20Acquermann.md). [Janna](Janna%20Furwish.md) claims that [Kariil Acquermann](Kariil%20Acquermann.md) presented [Laurie](Laurie%20Furwish.md) a sigil similar to what [Kristofer Acquermann](Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) gave to [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md).
- [Session 59](../Session%20Log/Session%2059.md): [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks about [Kariil](Kariil%20Acquermann.md). [Vanayra](Vanayra%20Metorne.md) mentions that he is the son of [Korvin Acquermann](Korvin%20Acquermann.md), and is the inheritor of [Acquermann Clan](Acquermann%20Clan.md)'s legacy. [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md) claims that he is the rightful heir to the [Acquermann Clan](Acquermann%20Clan.md)'s legacy.
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): The intruder's shadowy trappings dissipate as he enters the [Hall of Akenatun](Hall%20of%20Akenatun.md). The [party](Seven%20Up....md) realize that the intruder is [Korvin Acquermann](Korvin%20Acquermann.md), the long-lost brother of [Kayriel](Kayriel%20Acquermann.md) and [Kristofer](Kristofer%20Acquermann.md).
> *"I think it is time to reveal myself. I am [Korvin Acquermann](Korvin%20Acquermann.md). I heard that you are searching for me, [Kirren](Kirren%20Acquermann.md) and [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md)..."*
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): [Korvin Acquermann](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) talks to the [party](Seven%20Up....md). [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md) asks [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) whether he knows who is sieging [Kristofer](Kristofer%20Acquermann.md). [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) produces a sly smile; he answers that it was him who is assaulting [Kristofer](Kristofer%20Acquermann.md)'s domain.
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md) reacts and casts hold person on [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md).
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): [Korvin Acquermann](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) admits that he ordered the [Shadow Knight](Shadow%20Knight.md) to negotiate with the [party](Seven%20Up....md). He tells the [party](Seven%20Up....md) that [Roldan](Roldan%20Vinke.md) and [Elix](Elix%20Claymore.md) might provide answers where [Elvin](Elvin%20Claymore.md) is.
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): The [party](Seven%20Up....md) asks [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) about his allegiance to [[The Cult of Miledu|The Cult of Miledu]]. [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) shares that he used [Elvin](Elvin%20Claymore.md) and [[The Cult of Miledu|The Cult of Miledu]] to further his own goal. [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) appeared to help [Elvin](Elvin%20Claymore.md) in his bid to free [Miledu](Miledu.md), as he thought that the plan was impossible. [Elvin](Elvin%20Claymore.md)'s string of successes worries [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md), and now [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) appeals to the [party](Seven%20Up....md) to join forces and defeat [Elvin](Elvin%20Claymore.md) and [[The Cult of Miledu|The Cult of Miledu]] for good.
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): The [party](Seven%20Up....md) asks [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) about his goals. [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) promises to depose the gods and free the people of the world from their hold.
- [Session 80](../Session%20Log/Session%2080.md): [Kiris](Kiris%20Acquermann.md) and [Kirren](Kirren%20Acquermann.md) ask [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) about their lineage. [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) tells them that the [Acquermann Clan](Acquermann%20Clan.md) was the leader of the first humans who waged war against the gods during the [Age of Humans](Age%20of%20Humans.md), making the [Acquermann Clan](Acquermann%20Clan.md) as the 'most flawed clan.' After being defeated, [Akenatun](Akenatun.md) forgives the [Acquermann Clan](Acquermann%20Clan.md) and appoints them to protect his legacy. [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md) claims that this magnanimous act of mercy is just a show---an attempt by the gods to control people and retain power. This makes the gods flawed, in [Korvin](Korvin%20Acquermann.md)'s point of view.
