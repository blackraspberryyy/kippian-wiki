## Description
[Father Ignatius](Ignatius%20Lavell.md) is the young priest who tends to [Chapel of Aisef](../Locations/Buildings/Chapel%20of%20Aisef.md) in [Foenyx](../Locations/Areas/Foenyx%20District.md). He has little experience in serving as a cleric, but he has a lot of faith to [Aisef](../Deities/New%20Gods/Aisef.md).
