## Description
An arrowhead that can be worn similar to a soldier's emblem. Members of the [United Kippian Military](../Factions/United%20Kippian%20Military.md) gifts this to colleagues that they have a deep, personal relationship with.

The arrowhead is blessed by [Enchor](../Deities/New%20Gods/Enchor.md), and has a magical effect.
