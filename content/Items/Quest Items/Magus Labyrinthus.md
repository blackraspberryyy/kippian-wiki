## Description
An artifact with same powers as that of the [Arcanus Labyrinthus](../../../Arcanus%20Labyrinthus.md). Destroyed by [Kristofer](../../../Kristofer%20Acquermann.md) to permanently bind [Miledu](../../../Miledu.md) from [Darkcastle Mound](../../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md).
# Story Log
## Most Recent
- [Session 85](../../Session%20Log/Session%2085.md): The [party](Seven%20Up....md) enter an empty room save for a pedestal containing the destroyed remains of a spherical artifact. [Adikia](Adikia%20Unalome.md) immediately recognizes that it is the [Magus Labyrinthus](Magus%20Labyrinthus.md), which they saw from [Splix](Spraugh%20'Splix'%20Calix.md)'s magic.
- [Session 85](../../Session%20Log/Session%2085.md): [[Kristofer Acquermann|Kristofer]] confesses that in order to make his plan foolproof, [[Kristofer Acquermann|Kristofer]] destroyed the [Magus Labyrinthus](Magus%20Labyrinthus.md). This drastic action makes the binding permanent on the [[Darkcastle Mound|castle]]'s end. But the artifact also enables the [[Darkcastle Mound|castle]]'s portal sanctum; this means that [[Kristofer Acquermann|Kristofer]] has no way to teleport out of [Shadowfell](Shadowfell.md). He hides this fact from the [Wayravens](Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) in order to not weaken their morale.
- [Session 85](../../Session%20Log/Session%2085.md): [[Kristofer Acquermann|Kristofer]] shows his locket, revealing an Ymerald shard that was once part of the [Magus Labyrinthus](Magus%20Labyrinthus.md). [Adikia](Adikia%20Unalome.md) asks [[Kristofer Acquermann|Kristofer]] if he knows where the rest of their party is located considering that the location of the [Magus Labyrinthus](Magus%20Labyrinthus.md) should have been their destination. [[Kristofer Acquermann|Kristofer]] claims that he knows nothing about where their [party](Seven%20Up....md) are right now.
