## Description
Written on scrolls by the eponymous [Padwin Furwish](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md), [the notes](Padwin's%20Notes.md) record the lorekeeper's thoughts and research on a couple of subjects.
## Content
### About [Padwin Furwish](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md)
- [Padwin Furwish](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md) is an expert on the planes parallel to the [Material Plane](../Locations/Planes/Material%20Plane.md): [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md) and [Feywild](../Locations/Planes/Feywild.md).
- [Padwin Furwish](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md) is a close friend of the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md). [Padwin](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md) archives the exploits of the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md); however, the location of the records can only be accessed by those who "lit the light that reveals the truth."
### [Age of Kings](./Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Kings.md) and [Age of Heroes](./Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Heroes.md)
- Through their act of mercy, [Akenatun](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Akenatun.md) adopted and absolved the human race. Because of this, humans earned the ire of the two primal races, the elves and the dwarves.
- Borders are drawn between the three races. Kingdoms are founded, initiating the [Age of Kings](./Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Kings.md).
- Heroes emerge and bring glory to their patron kings. The emergence of these heroes would soon herald the [Age of Heroes](./Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Heroes.md).
### On [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md)
- [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) is built on a common intersection between the [Material Plane](../Locations/Planes/Material%20Plane.md) and [Shadowfell](../Locations/Planes/Shadowfell.md). This allows for easier travel between the two planes.
- [Darkcastle Mound](../Locations/Buildings/Darkcastle%20Mound.md) is a stronghold discovered or built by the [Wayravens](../Factions/Knights%20of%20the%20Wayraven.md) during the [Age of Heroes](./Eons%20and%20Ages/Age%20of%20Heroes.md).
### On [Miledu](../../Miledu.md)
- [Miledu](../../Miledu.md) is entombed into a deep stasis by [The Old Gods](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md).
- [Padwin](../../Padwin%20Furwish.md) ponders why [The Old Gods](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md) did not destroy[Miledu](../../Miledu.md). The historian believes that destroying [Miledu](../../Miledu.md) will trigger some sort of apocalypse.
- Due to a transgression by his son [Kortusk](../Deities/New%20Gods/Kortusk.md), [Macchi](../Deities/Old%20Gods/Macchi.md) is punished to watch over [Miledu](../../Miledu.md).
