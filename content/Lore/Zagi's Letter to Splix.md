## Description
> To the keeper of my staff,
>
> I now hold you responsible for this large deposit of Ymerald. I have also collected and recorded all that I know in this room. S, I entrust all of these to you.
>
> I foresee an enemy from the heavens... Maybe this extraterrestrial boon is not a gift, but a weapon to protect us against the things to come.
>
> But I digress. S, you and your companions must first defeat the evil that plans to release [Miledu](../../Miledu.md). The twelve chains that bind the harbinger of doom are weakening. But the god is vulnerable from its stasis; strike now before it awakens.
>
> [Zerrus Zagi](./Mythical%20Heroes/Zerrus%20Zagi.md),
> Creator and First Keeper of the [Ymerald Staff](../Items/Mythic%20Items/Ymerald%20Staff.md)
