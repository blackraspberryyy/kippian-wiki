## Description
Located on an island in [Foenyx](../Areas/Foenyx%20District.md), this magnanimous cathedral is dedicated to [The Old Gods](../../Deities/Old%20Gods/Pantheon%20of%20the%20Old%20Gods.md). It is also the home of [Stella Kari](../../NPCs/Stella%20Kari.md), [Matunda](../../Deities/Old%20Gods/Matunda.md)'s only cleric.
